Analyzing Texas' Executed Criminals

Texas is known for its harsh use of the death penalty. Since 1976, the state has carried out 515 executions (more than 37% of all US executions) and currently has 278 inmates on death row.

In this post, I would like to analyze a few things about those executed on Texas soil and allow the data to tell its own story. All graphics are done with D3. Sources are at the bottom.
